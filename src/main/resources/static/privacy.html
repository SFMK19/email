Fitness4life Personal Information Protection Policy

Personal Information Protection

Fitness4life is committed to protecting and respecting your personal information. This policy (together with any other policy notified on any part of this website from time to time) sets out the basis on which any personal information we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us.

Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information and how we will treat it. This policy may change from time to time and, if it does, the latest version will be available on our website.

Key Points

Fitness4life Fitness Services (Shanghai) Limited is the ‘Personal Information Processor’ under the Personal Information Protection Law (also known internationally as the data controller) of personal information processed through your use of our website.

The Information We Process

  • Information about our website visitors, which may include information provided using the ‘Contact Us’ form or forms required to download certain content, as well as technical information about your device, settings, and site use. We also process information that we receive from third parties, such as our website analytics provider and search engine optimizer.
  • Information that you provide when you want to become an instructor, as well as while you remain an instructor, including information provided by health and fitness clubs relating to your role as an instructor.
  • Information about employees and owners of health and fitness clubs when they use our services and our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data, amongst other things, to:

  • (If you are a website visitor) Answer your questions and provide you with access to our site and services, inform you about them, analyze and improve the effectiveness of our website.
  • (If you are an instructor) Provide you with information, training, and updates on new releases, assess your performance and distribute information relating to your role and certifications, and understand your views about Fitness4life.
  • (If you are an employee or owner of a health and fitness club) Provide you with information about your instructors, training, and new updates.
  • Comply with any legal or regulatory obligations, respond to law enforcement agencies, and conduct personal information security audits.

If you are an instructor, employee, or owner of a health and fitness club and you consent, we will send you newsletters relating to our services, which you can opt out of at any time by sending an email to legal@fitness4life.com or clicking unsubscribe at the bottom of any email.

You have a number of rights that you may exercise in relation to our use of your personal information. You may exercise these by emailing your request to legal@fitness4life.com.

Please contact us at legal@fitness4life.com with any questions or concerns about this policy.
